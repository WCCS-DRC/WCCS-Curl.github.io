About Us

We are a new start-up company* dedicated to developing tools that can support grassroots curling. From pace of play tools, to smart device ecosystems, our goal is to bring innovation to the recreational curler, recreation curling clubs and grassroots coaches, to add value, at afforable prices. We're just starting out, but we've got big plans.

Curling is one of the best activities on earth, truly.

  • It is accessible and affordable to most.
  • It offers individual and team challenge.
  • It combines mental focus and strategic thinking with raw physical exertion.
  • It is supportive of multigenerational participation.
  • It is social but can also be competitive

It's all these things and more but most of all, it's fun! Getting started is easy, getting better can be difficult. We want to help. We're curlers, helping curlers. Check out what we have to offer as we roll out new things.

Have an idea? Looking for something we don't have (or no-one seems to have)? Want to collaborate? Drop us a line! Let's talk curling.
